About

Muzec Predictor

Muzec Predictor is an advanced music genre classifier designed to automatically categorize and label audio tracks into specific genres. Leveraging machine learning algorithms and signal processing techniques, Muzec Predictor analyzes various audio features such as tempo, RMS Energy, Spectral Centroid, and chorma etc..., to determine the genre of a given track. Its predictive capabilities are refined through extensive training on diverse music datasets, allowing it to accurately classify music across a wide range of genres including blues, classical, country, disco, hip- hop, jazz, metal, pop, reggae, and rock. Muzec Predictor serves as a valuable tool for music recommendation systems, playlist generation, and music discovery platforms, offering users a seamless and personalized listening experience based on their genre preferences.
